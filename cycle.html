The Atlantic Seaboard Blast (32 km)

A stunning coastal route along the Atlantic Seaboard of **Cape Town**. This $32 \text{ km}$ loop starts at the **Sea Point Promenade**, passes through **Clifton** and **Camps Bay**, offering breathtaking views of the Twelve Apostles and Lion's Head. It's relatively flat for the first half, with a few manageable climbs near Camps Bay. Perfect for a morning ride before the traffic hits!

  • **Distance:** $32 \text{ km}$ (approximate)
  • **Elevation Gain:** $\approx 350 \text{ m}$
  • **Highlight:** Panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean and Table Mountain.
